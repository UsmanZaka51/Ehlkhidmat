Ehl-e-Khidmat Welfare organization

  • About
  • Mission
Ehl-e-Khidmat is a small platform, formed by a few students of Burewala in March 2020, that is trying to seek the betterment of the society by serving people in every possible manner.
Ehl-e-Khidmat welfare organization is in the field of social welfare for the last 2 years, continuously providing monthly rations to deserving families and all possible educational help to the poor and needy students who cannot afford educational expenditures. We also provide tuition fee, examination fee, registration fee and admission fee to needy and deserving students at school and college levels. We are hopeful to expand our network nationwide. Our mission is to end hunger and poverty in our society and create lasting solutions to overcome poverty.

Ehl-E-Khidmat Domain's

Donate For After Life

Healthy Food

We provide monthly ration to deserving Families. Most of them are widows with no source of income.

Health Care

We provide medicines & other medical assistance (Up to 5000/PKR) to needy patients who are unable to arrange by themselves.

Primary Education

We help School & College students (Preferably girls) to meet their monthly educational expenditures. We provide them with books, uniform and monthly fee.

Rozgar Scheme

We provide respectful livelihood to those who want to work so that, they can earn and feed their families.
