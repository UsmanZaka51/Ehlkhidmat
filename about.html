About Us

Ehl-E-Khidmat Welfare organization

  • About
In early 2020, all the students (founder and co-founders) were free from universities due to pandemic of Covid-19. In those days, the pandemic was at its peak. The life of every person was froze and it was expected that the progress of world had stopped and no one knows for how much time that disaster would last. A lot of people became jobless as most of the businesses were ceased at once. People were feeling entirely helpless. In those sequences of life, a few friends (less than 10) sat together and decided to do something productive because doing something useful is far way better than doing nothing. They decided to do something that would help the hopeless people. They decided to overcome the hunger. Ehl-e-Khidmat was established on 18th March 2020.
In that case they planned to feed a family of a widow who was unable to earn and feed her children. The journey began with giving ration to that one family. Those friends collected one hundred rupees each and bought ration for that family. After that they wished to help more deserving people, especially the families who have no source of income. In that way, each of them contacted their family members and relatives and informed them of their noble intentions. Along with that, they started bringing their classmates along with them. As a result, some new students joined Ehl-e-Khidmat, we were able to provide ration to more than one family. This journey slowly continued on its way and one by one members joined us. As the number of students increased, we added more families and provided them with ration. The donations were entirely from the pocket money of students. Our priority was to help widows who had no breadwinner to support their families.
Apart from this, we also included some families of other than widows and orphans, who were really in need. In the same way, this series continued and by the Grace of Allah, today, the number of our members is increasing and we are trying to help the deserving people as much as possible. If the intention of a person is good, Allah paves the way for him. So if you also want to help people around you then join us.

We are working in these domains:

1) Ehl-e-Khidmat Ration Program
In Ration program, we provide monthly ration to deserving Families. Most of them are widows with no source of income (Range 3500-4000/PKR).
2) Education For All
In this Program, we help School & College students (Preferably girls) to meet their monthly educational expenditures. We provide them with books, uniform and monthly fee up to 5000/PKR.
3) Healthy Pakistan
In this program, we provide medicines & other medical assistance (Up to 5000/PKR) to needy patients who are unable to arrange by themselves.
4) Ehl-e-Khidmat Small Rozgar Scheme
In our Rozgar Scheme, we provide respectful livelihood to those who want to work so that, they can earn and feed their families. The range of this project is up to 30,000/PKR.
5) Plantation Drive
We encourage the plantation of different kinds of flowers and plants to make our environment pleasant. We aim for Neat and Clean Pakistan.
6) Ehl-e-khidmat Dastar-khan (Ramadan)
We provide fasting people with Iftaar meals annually in Ramadan Karim. We try to cover as much people as possible. Along with this, we distribute about 150+ Iftar boxes every week.
7) Warm clothes drive
In every winter season, warm clothes are distributed among needy people who cannot afford to cover themselves.
8) Ehl-e-khidmat shelter program
We work every possible way to provide shelter to homeless people. We are currently working on building houses for the flood affected people.
For any new case, the team members themselves go and review everything. After full satisfaction, if the case is really genuine, it is added in our list. Otherwise it is rejected.
Progress reports of all the teams of Ehl-e-Khidmat are made every month, are sent to all members and donors so that all stakeholders can be aware of our monthly performance.
The team would like to help more and more deserving people in the future. And our goal is not to let any person around us go hungry and helpless.
We are hopeful to expand our network nationwide. Our mission is to end hunger and poverty in our society and create lasting solutions to overcome poverty. Let's help people to eradicate the hunger together. We pray to Almighty Allah to give Ehl-e-Khidmat more progress so that our help can cover more deserving people.
